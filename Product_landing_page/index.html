Magic mouse

VictSing wired mouse comes with 10 buttons that can be fully programmed via the included CD drive. You can reassign each of its buttons to a different function or macro so as to make your mouse control faster and more proficient. You can also save one programming mode for gaming and one for web surfing and switch these 2 modes via the bottom button.

Features

AI powered

High-Precision Tracking: The mouse uses advanced laser technology for precise movement and control. Works with personal computers for smooth navigation and control. .

For coders

Smooth rubberized scroll wheel and sweat-resistant matte finish offer you effortless and secure grip. Prevent from hand fatigue during intense gaming. The mouse operates on a 2.4GHz wireless connection for convenient use.
